💡 Why These Algorithms?

Our reasoning for model selection

🌲
Random Forest
Builds 100 independent decision trees and averages results. Resistant to overfitting, handles non-linear patterns well, and naturally provides feature importance rankings.
⚡
Gradient Boosting
Builds trees sequentially where each tree corrects the errors of the previous one. Excellent for complex patterns but requires careful tuning to prevent overfitting.
🚀
XGBoost
Optimized gradient boosting with L1/L2 regularization. Industry standard for tabular data competitions. Faster training with built-in handling of missing values.
